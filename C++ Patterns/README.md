C++ Pattern Programs 🔷
A collection of C++ pattern programs using nested loops — perfect for beginners learning loop logic and pattern building.

📋 Patterns List
#	File	Pattern Name	Description
01	01_alphabet_triangle.cpp	Alphabet Triangle	Prints A, AB, ABC... on each row
02	02_continuous_alphabet_grid.cpp	Continuous Alphabet Grid	Prints alphabets continuously across rows
03	03_left_aligned_star_triangle.cpp	Left Aligned Star Triangle	Grows from 1 star to n stars
04	04_inverted_star_triangle.cpp	Inverted Star Triangle	Shrinks from n stars to 1 star
05	05_hourglass_star.cpp	Hourglass Star	Inverted triangle on top + normal triangle below
06	06_right_aligned_growing_triangle.cpp	Right Aligned Growing Triangle	Stars grow and align to the right
07	07_right_aligned_shrinking_triangle.cpp	Right Aligned Shrinking Triangle	Stars shrink with right alignment
08	08_right_aligned_spaced_star_triangle.cpp	Right Aligned Spaced Star Triangle	Stars with spaces, right aligned
09	09_left_star_triangle.cpp	Left Star Triangle	Grows from 1 star to n stars (variant)
10	10_inverted_star_triangle_v2.cpp	Inverted Star Triangle v2	Shrinks from n stars to 1 (variant)
11	11_hourglass_star_v2.cpp	Hourglass Star v2	Inverted + Normal triangle (variant)
12	12_right_growing_triangle.cpp	Right Growing Triangle	Stars grow, aligned right (variant)
13	13_right_shrinking_triangle.cpp	Right Shrinking Triangle	Stars shrink, aligned right (variant)
14	14_right_spaced_triangle.cpp	Right Spaced Triangle	Stars with spaces, right aligned (variant)
15	15_diamond_pyramid.cpp	Diamond Pyramid	Centered growing star pyramid
16	16_full_diamond.cpp	Full Diamond	Complete diamond top + bottom
